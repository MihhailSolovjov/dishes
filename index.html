My Favorite Dishes

Mihhail Solovjov

    Pancakes with meat
    Milk 1L
    Eggs 2pcs
    Vegetable oil 7 tablespoons
    Flour 425g
    Salt 1 teaspoon
    Soda 0.5 teaspoon
    Minced pork or chicken 400g
    Bulb onions 1 pcs
  1. Pancake
    Omelette
    Eggs 2pcs
    Sausages 2pcs
    Tomato 2pcs
    Garlic 2 cloves
    Salt - to taste
    Parsley to taste
    Vegetable oil for frying
  1. Omelette

Cooking pancakes with meat

    Peel, wash and cut onion into cubes.
    Preheat a frying pan. Pour in vegetable oil. Lay out the onion. Fry over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until golden brown, Put the minced meat on the onion. Fry, stirring occasionally, until tender, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Mix. Refrigerate.
    Pour warm milk into a bowl. Break the eggs there. Add salt and sugar. Beat with a whisk or mixer. Add butter and flour, beat until smooth.
    Boil the kettle. Pour baking soda into a glass, pour boiling water over (3 tablespoons). Pour baking soda into a bowl of dough. Stir well. Let the dough stand for 10 minutes.
    Preheat a frying pan. Add 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil. Pour half a ladle of dough into the center of the pan. Spread the dough over the entire surface of the pan. As soon as the top side of the pancake is no longer liquid, turn the pancake over.
    Then turn over and bake on the other side for 30-40 seconds.
    So bake all the pancakes.
    Put the meat filling on the pancake, roll the pancake into an envelope. Pancakes with meat can be served.
    Serve the meat pancakes with sour cream.

Cooking Omelette

    Pour vegetable oil into a frying pan, place on the stove and heat slightly. Peel the sausages from the film, cut into slices and put them in a frying pan.
    Peel the garlic, wash and chop finely with a knife. When the sausages are golden, send the chopped garlic to them.
    Wash the tomatoes, cut them into half rings and put them in the pan immediately.
    Cook everything together for about 1 minute. During this time, break the eggs into a bowl, add washed and chopped parsley to them, add salt.
    Whisk everything with a fork.
    Pour the resulting egg mass into everything in the pan, cover the eggs with sausages and tomatoes with a lid and simmer all this on a tiny flame for a couple of minutes.
    You can sprinkle the cooked eggs with tomatoes and sausages with fresh herbs and serve. Enjoy your meal!
